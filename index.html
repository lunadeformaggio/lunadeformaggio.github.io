GETTING TO KNOW ME

Hello, I'm Aying! A self-supporting IV - BS Geography student at University of the Philippines and a passionate freelance map-maker.

I am an international mapathon champion. I am expert in creating detailed location maps for various applications and producing hazard maps to assess and visualize potential risks. Also an experienced in flood analysis mapping for identifying flood-prone areas and in developing suitability maps to determine the best locations for specific activities or developments.

As a map-maker, I transform ideas into visual landscapes. My love for cartography and attention to detail drive me to create custom maps tailored to the unique needs of my clients.

I create maps using QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, Adobe Illustrator, Blender, and RStudio.
